Dues Information

An application fee of $5.00 is required with all applications. [cite: 222] Dues are prorated by quarter.

  • Full Dues (18+): $21.00 per year [cite: 219]
  • Under 18: $5.00 per year (not prorated) [cite: 222]
  • Family Plan: Full dues for first member, 1/2 dues for each additional family member in the same household. [cite: 53]
  • Prorated Dues (First Year): [cite: 220]
    • Jan/Feb/Mar: $21.00
    • Apr/May/Jun: $15.75
    • Jul/Aug/Sep: $10.50
  • Note: Applications in Oct/Nov/Dec pay the $5.00 fee + full dues for the *next* year ($26.00 total). [cite: 221]
