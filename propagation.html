Pro Tips from W3BN

  • 40m & 80m light up after local sunset — perfect for ragchews
  • 20m opens to EU ~1300–1900 UTC in winter
  • Check A-index < 15 for clean DX
  • Solar flux > 120? 10m might be open!
  • Use the W3BN Net to test conditions
